In my role, I oversaw and enhanched the operations of the Goodwill Computer Works department, located in the heart of South Austin. At the core of our mission was the responsible management of a diverse range of electronics, spanning from cutting-edge gadgets to timeless tech relics. Our team's dedication went beyond the mere management of products and led to a commitment to sustainability, community impact, etc. Through our efforts, we not only kept electronics out of landfills but also provided access to affordable, reliable technology for families in need.
